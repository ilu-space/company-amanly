How It Works

Set up in minutes. Connected for good.

No technical expertise needed. Amanly is designed to be simple, transparent, and respectful of everyone in the family.

1
Parent creates a family account

Sign up with your email, choose your language (English or Arabic), and create your family profile. Takes under two minutes.

2
Children accept the invitation

Your child receives an invite, installs the Amanly app, and explicitly accepts the connection. A visible indicator on their device confirms the link — no hidden monitoring.

3
Stay connected & informed

Chat, check device status, and review files — all with full transparency. Your child always knows what's shared. You always know they're safe.
